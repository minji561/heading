Bob parr(Mr.Incredible)

Robert "Bob" Parr(a.k.a Mr.Incredible) possesses superhuman strength, stamina, and durability. He is married to Helen Parr, the superheroine known as Elastigirl, and they have three children together. His red superhero suit, designed by Edna Mode, appears to have the same level of durability as Mr.Incredible himself. Mr.Incredible was ranked number 5 in IGN's list of the Top 10 pixar Characters.
